Our Model

Three Sections,
Maximum Efficiency

We segment our livestock for specialized care, ensuring every animal gets exactly what it needs.

Breeding Section

Female goats (nannies) and strong males selected for optimal reproduction. Carefully managed genetics ensure healthy offspring and herd growth.

Meat Section

Mature, well-nourished goats ready for consumption and premium quality. Perfect for households, restaurants, and bulk food vendors.

Kids Section

Healthy young goats for rearing with carefully monitored growth programs. Ideal for farmers looking to grow their own herds affordably.
